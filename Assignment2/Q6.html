Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g grated parmesan
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Cook the spaghetti in boiling salted water until al dente.
  2. Fry the pancetta until crispy.
  3. Beat the eggs and mix with grated parmesan.
  4. Drain the spaghetti and combine with pancetta and egg mixture.
  5. Season with salt and pepper, and serve hot.

Nutritional Facts

Calories: 400 per serving

Protein: 15g

Carbohydrates: 50g

Fat: 10g

Fun Fact: Carbonara is a traditional Italian dish from the Lazio region.
